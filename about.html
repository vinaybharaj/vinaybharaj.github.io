About

Operator first, consultant second.

I've been the planner missing the forecast, the consultant redesigning the process, and the owner answering for the P&L.

I started on the shop floor of the number. At a $200M retailer I ran demand planning, re-engineered idea-to-store, and owned a $5M e-commerce P&L, reporting to the founders' office. That's why my transformations get adopted: I design for the planner who has to trust the system on a Tuesday morning.

Then I crossed the table. At Accenture Strategy I led AI forecasting rollouts, ML replenishment, control towers, and zero-based cost programs for global retail, CPG, and pharma — delivered on the ground across five continents.

Today I lead Data & AI engagements as a Senior Manager at Sigmoid, advising CXOs on planning, supply chain analytics, and AI for operations.
